
H1 Keep in mind
Don't be afraid to try out new ways of parenting. Putting these ideas into practice with your teenager takes time.Be patient.
If you feel you've made some parenting mistakes, don't be too hard on yourself. You can always learn from these mistakes.Be kind to yourself.
If your teenager has already developed depression or clinical anxiety, don't feel that it is a failure on your part or blame yourself. Any teenager can develop these problems.Just by completing this module, you are already taking steps in the right direction towards helping your teenager.
