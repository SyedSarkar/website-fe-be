
H1 Good social skills
To help your teenager develop good social skills (and good friends), you can:
- Encourage your teenager to do kind things for others.
- Set an example for your teenager by the way you communicate in social situations and manage social challenges. For tips on assertive communication, see theCalm versus Conflictmodule.
- Show your teenager healthy friendships by having good friends who are trustworthy, loyal and fun aroundyou!
- Talk through any social difficulties or friendship troubles that your teenager may have. For tips on managing friendship troubles and problem solving, see thePartners in Problem Solvingmodule.
