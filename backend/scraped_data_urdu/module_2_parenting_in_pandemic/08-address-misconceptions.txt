The pandemic situation and local regulations are constantly changing. You don’t have to have it all figured out.
- If there are questions you cannot answer, be honest and let your teen know that you will try your best to find out the answers, and then make time to talk with them again.
- If you can’t find out the answers, acknowledge that some questions cannot be answered at this time because we are still learning about the pandemic.
Remember, it’s not all about the pandemic - keep up the normal everyday conversations and activities with your teen.
