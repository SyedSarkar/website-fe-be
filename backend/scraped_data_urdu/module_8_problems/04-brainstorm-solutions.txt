
H1 Effective problem-solving

H3 2. Brainstorm possible solutions.
Help your teenager to come up with as many solutions to the problem as possible bybrainstorming.

H3 Here are some brainstorming tips:
- Try to create a free-flowing exchange of ideas between you and your teenager.
- Think as creatively as possible.
- Write down all the ideas.
- Don’t stop to evaluate the ideas – just come up with as many suggestions as you can think of.
- Try to come up with at least 5 possible solutions.

H3 For example, in response to the problem “I need to improve my marks in Maths otherwise I will not pass the subject”, possible solutions could include:
Get a Maths tutor.
Do Maths exercises each night as homework.
Get extra help from the teacher.
Have a study partner.
Ask for study guides.
