
H2 Emotions during the pandemic
Have you noticed your teen is more emotional during the pandemic?
[IMAGE: emotion-words.png - Emotive words]
All of the above emotions are normal and expected. Teenagers are likely to feel a range of strong emotions during this time. Your teen’s emotional response will depend on their age, maturity, their understanding of the pandemic, the response of adults around them (including you and other family members), their personality, and their coping skills. Let your teen know that it’s normal and okay to feel these emotions.
Some teens might express their emotions through challenging behaviours, for example defiance or acting out. If you’ve found your teen’s behaviour has changed during the pandemic, keep in mind that this could be their way of expressing strong emotions. Remember, your teen is trying to navigate all of the challenges of the pandemic, while also developing the ability to express and cope with their emotions.
