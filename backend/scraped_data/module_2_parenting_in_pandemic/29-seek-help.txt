
H2 Seek help if needed
Remember to seek professional support for yourself and/or your teen if needed.
Consider seeking mental health support if:
- Your teen has major difficulties adjusting to challenges of the pandemic.
- You are struggling with your own mental health.
By seeking support for yourself when needed, you are also setting a good example for your teen.
If you’d like to learn more about supporting your teen during the pandemic, these PiP modules also have some COVID-19 information:
- Connect- includes supporting your teen emotionally during the pandemic
- Calm versus conflict- includes coping with increased family conflict during the pandemic
- Good health habits for good mental health- includes screen time, physical activity, nutrition, and sleep.
