H6 Select a goal to focus on this week
Please select one goal from the list below that you think is achievable for you over the next week. Your goals will be available on your dashboard, where you can mark them as complete. After completing your goal, you can return to the module to select additional goals if you wish.
Click theicon to select your goal. Remove a goal with theicon.
- Pick one self-care activity that you could do more of this week.Think about when and how you can fit this into your schedule. SeePrioritising self-care.
- Have a chat with your teen about what worries them most about the pandemic.Focus on listening and understanding their concerns (seeHave the conversationsandCoping with worries and uncertainties).
- Work with your teen to develop their ideal daily routine.Write this down and try to support your teen to stick to it for the week. SeeCreating healthy routines.
- Reflect on the expectations you have of your teenager.Think about whether any of these need adjusting during the pandemic. SeeManaging your expectations.
- Have a chat with your teen about where they are getting the latest information about the pandemic.Discuss with your teen how reliable this information is. See the tips onFinding the right information.
