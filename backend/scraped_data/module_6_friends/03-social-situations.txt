
H1 A range of social situations
Encourage your teenager to be involved in a range of social situations. Some good social activities include:
Language classes
Singing in a choir
Playing music in a band
Debating team
Paid and unpaid jobs
Martial arts
Team Sport (soccer, netball, cricket, swimming team, football, basketball, hockey, tennis, volleyball… the list is endless)
Athletics Club
Scouts
Cadets
Guides
Drama
Gymnastics
Dance
Of course, your teenager does not need to doallthese activities at any point in time (and neither should they)! The key is to encourage them to try different things, so that they get to experience different social situations.
