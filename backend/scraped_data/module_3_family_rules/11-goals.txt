H6 The goal you have selected for this week is...
- Set aside some time to discuss rules and consequences with your teenager.If you don’t have any clear rules or consequences for your teen’s behaviour, set aside some time to discuss this with them (SeeHow).

H6 Think about:
- When will this happen?
- Who will be involved?
- What might get in the way of this happening?
[IMAGE: goalArrow-70258fb7e13afd57c7eda26da23ce71e.svg]
- Review your existing rules and consequences.See if any of your existing rules or consequences need to be adjusted to give your teen more freedom (SeeReviewing rules).
- Thank your teenager for their good behaviour.Tell them you appreciate it, and try to do something special for them (SeeCatch 'em being good).
- Think of a rule for your teen’s behaviour that doesn’t have clear consequences.Together with your teenager, set some specific and realistic consequences (SeeHowandApplying the rules).
