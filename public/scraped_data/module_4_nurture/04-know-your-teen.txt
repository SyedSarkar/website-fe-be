
H1 How well do you know your teenager?
Here is an exercise to help you get to know your teenager's world better.
Do you know what your teenager enjoys doing in their spare time (e.g. listening to music, gaming, hanging out with friends, kicking the football)?
Yes
No
It's great to know what your teenager is doing in their spare time and to encourage them to do positive activities that they enjoy.
Do you know what time of day your teenager prefers to spend time with you (e.g. after school, dinner time, weekend afternoons)?
Teenagers aren't always ready to talk when you are. Knowing what time of day suits them will help you choose the best time to approach them.
Do you know who your teenager's friends are?
Friends are a huge part of your teenager's life. Knowing who they are and what they do together helps you understand their influence on your teenager's life.
Do you know how your teenager is performing at school?
While it's common for parents to want their teenager to excel at school, it's important to encourage their efforts regardless of their results.
Do you know your teenager's Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Twitter, and/or Reddit account name?
Knowing your teenager's cyberworld is just as important as knowing their physical world. Talking to them about what they like to do online is a good way to start (seePartners in Problem Solvingmodule).
