
H1 Friendship troubles
Talking about friendship troubles is complex.How can I help?

H3 Listen
Take the time to listen to your teenager. Talking about friendship troubles can be hard. A few ways to encourage them to open up are some one-on-one time, car drives, texting, or even an email.

H3 Ask questions
It’s tempting to justtellyour teenager how to resolve their friendship troubles. But it’s more useful to encourage them to come up with a solution, with your support. Try asking questions in a curious way, to help your teenager problem solve their difficulties. Click on the parent image below to see some suggestions.
