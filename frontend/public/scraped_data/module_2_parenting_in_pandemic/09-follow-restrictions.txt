Here are some other tips to consider:
- Be a good role model:- Follow the local regulations and restrictions yourself.- Model flexibility and problem-solving by showing your teen how you adjust your daily life in response to changing regulations and restrictions.
- If your teen is sceptical about the risks of the pandemic, discuss information from reliable sources with them.
