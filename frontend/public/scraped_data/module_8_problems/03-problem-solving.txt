
H1 Effective problem-solving
Here are six problem-solving steps you can share with your teenager to help them deal with life’s problems.

H3 1. Define the problem.
Help your teenager to identify and clearly state the problem that they are facing. Encourage them to be specific about the behaviour, situation, timing, and circumstances that make it a problem.
Your teenager may need helpbreaking down their probleminto smaller, more manageable pieces, before addressing them one at a time.
