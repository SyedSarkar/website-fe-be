H6 The goal you have selected for this week is...
- Talk with your teen about a problem they are experiencing and work with them to apply the 6 problem-solving steps.See Effective problem-solving:1. Define the problem,2. Brainstorm possible solutions,3. Evaluate the possible solutions,4. Decide on a solution,5. Put the plan into actionand6. Evaluate the outcome).

H6 Think about:
- When will this happen?
- Who will be involved?
- What might get in the way of this happening?
[IMAGE: goalArrow-70258fb7e13afd57c7eda26da23ce71e.svg]
- Practise one stress management technique with your teenager.SeeStress management skills.
- Talk with your teen about the pressures they are under from school, sporting clubs, media, friends and family.Discuss with them how they are managing these pressures and how you can support them (SeeManaging pressures and expectations).
- Consider how you can adjust your expectations of your teen to reduce any excessive pressure they might feel.How well do these expectations match your teen’s individual personality and capabilities? Have an open chat with your teen about your reflections. SeeManaging pressures and expectations.
