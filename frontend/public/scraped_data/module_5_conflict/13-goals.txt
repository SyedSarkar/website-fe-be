H6 The goal you have selected for this week is...
- Reflect on how you yourself were treated by your parents.Do you see your parents in your own behaviour? How did you feel as a teenager being parented this way? Pick one positive parenting behaviour you will do more of this week, and one negative behaviour you will do less of (SeeNo end to the arguments).

H6 Think about:
- When will this happen?
- Who will be involved?
- What might get in the way of this happening?
