
H1 3. Talking through the tough stuff
It's common for teenagers to experience strong emotions. They might look overly worried or so angry they look like they are about to explode. In order to manage their emotions effectively, teenagers need to first learn how to recognise and understand them. Here are some tips on how you can help your teenager to do this.
H4 Identify
- Label the emotion.
H4 Validate
- Acknowledge your teenager's feelings about the situation.
H4 Understand
- Explore what has led to the emotions.
