
H1 Support = Acceptance + Confidence
The good news is, there are more helpful ways to respond to your teenager’s
anxiety! Watch how Jenny responds to Liliana’s anxiety in this video:
Notice how Jenny acknowledges Liliana’s anxiety (acceptance), but did
not ‘take over’. Instead, she remains empathic and calm, and supports Liliana
to face her anxiety and problem solve the situation, while expressingconfidencein Liliana’s ability to do so.
This combination of acceptance + confidence is the kind of supportive response
that can empower teenagers.2Over time, it builds their confidence
and skills in facing their fears and overcoming their anxiety.
