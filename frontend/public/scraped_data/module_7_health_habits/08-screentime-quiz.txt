What about increased screen time during the pandemic? (click to reveal)
It’s unavoidable that your teen’s screen time will increase at times during the pandemic. In fact, this will likely be necessary for your teen to stay connected with their peers and continue with activities that are important to them.
If your teen is spending more time online, try to monitor how this is affecting them. Most importantly, ensure it’s not causing them any harm — for example, are they seeing material that’s making them more anxious? What about cyberbullying, or risk of online sexual exploitation? Now more than ever, it’s important to check in with your teen about how they are spending their time online.
Don’t forget screen-free time.Even if local restrictions limit time spent out of the house, it’s important to spend time each day away from our devices. Your teen might need your encouragement — brainstorm some options with them (e.g. crafts, reading, playing with a pet).
