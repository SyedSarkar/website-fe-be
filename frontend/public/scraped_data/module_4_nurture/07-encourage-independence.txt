
H1 Encourage independence
Research shows that being involved in your teenager's life helps protect them from depression and clinical anxiety. However, being over-involved can increase their risk of developing depression and clinical anxiety1.
You can avoid becoming over-involved in your teenager's life by promoting their autonomy or independence. You can do this by:
Encouraging them to take on more responsibilities around the house.
Allowing them to do more things on their own over time.
Checking that your teenager is not too dependent on you.
